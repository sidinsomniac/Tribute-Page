History

Walter Hartwell White was born on September 7th, 1958
Studied at the California Institute of Technology specializing in X-ray crystallography
Sold his share of the company to Elliott Schwartz for $5,000
Worked in Application Labs. Also worked in a chemical lab near Los Alamos, and met his wife Skyler White
Became a chemistry teacher at JP Wynne High School where his son, Walter White Jr., also attended as a student
Was martyred on September 7th, 2010 due to a fatal gunshot wound, for trying to provide for his family
